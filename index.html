About Juemin

Summary

Juemin is a self-motivated professional with passion on web development. With a non-technical background, Juemin decided to learn about the industry and soon found herself very passionate about it. Now she’s equipped with three full-stacks’ coding skill 2sets, has several ongoing projects deployed on AWS, and is looking for a position on which she can learn more as a full-stack or front end developer.

Why switch to IT industry?

In 2014, Juemin got a master's degree of Public Administration at University of Washington (Seattle). With no STEM background, it was never easy getting to the decision of an entire career change to the IT industry. A chance at Seattle Glove Inc became Juemin's career turning point. In November 2015, she had a conversation with her supervisor and decided to rebuild the company's 20 years old website, which had hundreds of products' information hard coded - far away from a modern website. Juemin started learning web development techniques since then. In two months, she created a MySQL database holding the product information as well as rebuilt the website with PHP on back end.

During this task, Juemin found herself very passionate about coding. So Juemin attended a coding bootcamp at CodingDojo in June 2016. It is an intense 14-week program that trains students to become self-sufficient developers. Three full stacks are introduced during the time with a large amount of individual/group projects (GitHub based) assigned, which helps students learn from real practice.

Juemin learned three stacks at coding dojo: Python (on Django or Flask), Ruby on Rails, and MEAN (MongoDB, Express, AngularJS, NodeJS), and got three Black Belts for all stacks.
